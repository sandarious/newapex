Our Services

We Proudly Serve Individuals And Businesses In Lagos Mainland LGA Currently, With A View Of Expanding To More LGAs In Lagos, With Their Dirt And Waste Product Needs, Majorly Domestic Waste. Whether You Are A One Time Or Regular Waste Disposer, We Are Here To Cater For Your Needs. We Also Do Cleanup Services For Corporate And Non Corporate Organizations, Individuals And Residences.

One-time Evacuation

Under our one time evacuation service, you don't necessarily have to be our subcriber to engage us in disposing your waste. Just a click and one of our agents will get through to you promptly.

Regular Evacuation

You can subscribe on our platform to have a routine and scheduled disposal of your waste and also an opportunity to get a discount.

Clean Up

We also engage in cleaning services for corporate and non corporate organizations, halls, individuals and residence.

About

We are Troway, a private waste management company dedicated to environmental protection.

  • September 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our Idea was conceived while brainstorming on a social enterprise we can embark on to improve our scoiety. The environment is all we have! We could not allow it to keep degrading by the day.

  • January 2020

    An Agency is Born

    Officially established Towards the end of January 2020, and we have been actively trying to evacuate as much dirt as possible in the locations we serve

  • MISSION

    Our mission is to clean every area in Lagos one LGA at a time. We seek to actively fulfil the SDG goals Number 3 - Good health and well being, Number 6 - Clean water and sanitation, Number 11- Sustainable cities and communities

  • VISION

    Our vision is to become the most effective Waste management and disposal service in Nigeria
